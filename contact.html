Quick answers

Frequently asked questions.

How much does a website cost?

It depends on what you need — a landing page, a full business site or an online store are very different builds. Tell us your goals and budget and we’ll give you a clear, itemised quote with no surprises. Most projects start from a free consultation.

How long does a project take?

A standard business website typically takes 2–4 weeks from kickoff to launch. Online stores, custom systems and AI agents vary with scope — we’ll give you a timeline up front and keep you updated at every step.

Do you work with businesses outside Nairobi?

Yes — we work with clients across Kenya and beyond. Most of our process runs smoothly over WhatsApp, email and video calls, so distance is never a problem.

Can you add M-Pesa payments to my website?

Absolutely. We integrate M-Pesa (and card payments) into online stores, booking systems and even WhatsApp AI agents, so your customers can pay the way they already prefer.

What is a WhatsApp AI agent, exactly?

It’s an intelligent assistant that lives on your WhatsApp number. It answers customer questions in English and Swahili, qualifies leads, books appointments and can take M-Pesa payments — 24/7, even while you sleep.

Do you offer support after launch?

Yes. Hosting, maintenance, security updates, content changes and ongoing SEO or marketing — we offer flexible support retainers so your site keeps working as hard as you do.
